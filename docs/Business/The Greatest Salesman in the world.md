# The Greatest Salesman in the world by OG Mandino

it’s story of camel boy who got 10 scrolls from his master. this scroll contain rules to become best sales man in the world. first 6 chapter only include story how this camel boy got his scroll with specific terms and conditions.

1. I WILL ADAPT GOOD HABITS.
    1. this part mostly talk about embrace the change and adapting good habits
    2. this chapter mostly deal with this by taking everything positively in life
    3. generatelly just trying to be consistent and discipline 
2. I WILL PERSIST,UNTILL I WILL WIN.
    1. this one is very obvious
3. I AM NATURE'S GREATEST MIRACLE.
    1. this is about accepting your personal strength or uniqueness
4. I WILL THIS DAY AS IF IT IS MY LAST.
    1. this is about staying in your present
    2. don’t worry to much about your past mistakes, failures 
    3. also keep pushing your limits in whatever you do exercise, work, love
5. I WILL BE MASTER OF MY EMOTIONS.
    1. this mostly about dealing with your emotion
    2. control your emotion for your benefits
6. I WILL LAUGH AT THE WORLD....THIS THIS SHALL PASS...
    1. cultivate the habit of laughter
    2. don’t take your life too seriously
    3. be patient with your problems, time will solve everything
    4. just be happy
7. I WILL MULTIPLY MY VALUE A HUNDREDFOLD.
    1. basically focus on using leverage
    2. figure out extract or provide more value out of everything
8. I WILL ACT NOW.
